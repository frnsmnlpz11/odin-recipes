Pancit Bihon

For a long prosperous life!

An essential filipino dish for parties! to wish good luck to the celebrant, one of the most well known dishes in the Philippines. Using thin translucent rice noodles to soak up those juices! Credits to Maggi for this recipe!

Ingredients:

  • 2 pcs Chicken Breast
  • 1.5 cups water
  • 2 tablespoons canola and corn oil
  • 4 cloves garlic
  • 1 pc onion
  • 2 sachet MAGGI® Magic Sarap® 8g
  • 0.25 tablespoon of ground pepper
  • 150 grams bihon
  • 1 pc carrot
  • 1 cup string beans
  • 1 cup cabbage
  • 2 tablespoons spring onion

Steps:

  1. Simmer chicken in water for 10 minutes. Set aside the chicken and reserve the broth.
  2. Saut√© garlic and onion in oil. Pour broth, simmer and season with MAGGI® MAGIC SARAP® and pepper.
  3. Drain bihon and stir in the broth. Cover and cook for 5 minutes.
  4. Shred cooked chicken. Stir in chicken and vegetables in the noodles. Transfer on a serving plate and garnish with spring onion.
